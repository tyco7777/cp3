Responsible Use of Artificial Intelligence at CBU

California Baptist University is committed to the ethical, secure, and mission‑aligned use of artificial intelligence (AI). This policy establishes university‑wide expectations for the responsible use of AI technologies in teaching, learning, research, and operational activities.

AI use at CBU must reflect Christian values, uphold human dignity, protect students and employees, and comply with applicable legal, regulatory, accreditation, and contractual requirements.

Purpose

This policy promotes appropriate innovation while safeguarding sensitive information, maintaining academic and professional integrity, and ensuring accountability for decisions and outcomes supported by AI technologies.

Scope

This policy applies to all faculty, staff, students, contractors, and affiliates who use AI tools for university‑related tasks, projects, communications, or decision‑making activities.

Guiding Principles

  • Human accountability: Responsibility for decisions, approvals, communications, and outcomes remains with individuals—not AI systems.
  • Data protection: Confidential, regulated, and personally identifiable information must be protected according to university standards.
  • Accuracy and verification: AI outputs must be reviewed, validated, and corrected before use.
  • Transparency: Meaningful use of AI must be disclosed consistent with course, departmental, or project expectations.
  • Fairness and dignity: AI should promote equitable treatment and avoid bias or unjust outcomes.

Acceptable and Appropriate Use

AI tools may be used to support drafting, summarization, analysis, and assistive tasks when used in accordance with this policy, approved‑tool guidance, and applicable data‑classification rules.

AI systems must not serve as the sole source of truth or replace professional judgment or institutional responsibility.

Governance and Oversight

Oversight of AI adoption and use at CBU is provided by the CBU AI Oversight Committee, which reviews tools, monitors practices, and issues recommendations to support safe and effective AI integration.
