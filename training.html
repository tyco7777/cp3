AI Training and Related Resources

California Baptist University encourages faculty, staff, and students to develop foundational knowledge and practical skills for the responsible use of artificial intelligence. Training supports effective adoption, ethical judgment, and alignment with university policy.

The resources below provide recommended starting points for learning how to use Microsoft Copilot and related AI tools in ways that protect data, ensure accuracy, and maintain human accountability.

Recommended Microsoft Copilot Training

Microsoft provides free, high‑quality training designed for higher education and enterprise environments. These resources emphasize secure use, verification of outputs, and responsible AI practices.

    A beginner‑friendly learning path covering Copilot fundamentals, how it works across Microsoft applications, and best practices for responsible use.

  • Microsoft 365 Copilot Training – Explore All

Expectations for Training and Readiness

Users are encouraged to complete appropriate training before using AI tools for university‑related work. Training supports responsible use, helps reduce risk, and strengthens confidence when reviewing and verifying AI‑generated outputs.

Additional CBU‑specific training materials, workshops, and guidance may be introduced as AI capabilities and institutional use continue to evolve.
