Responsible Use of Artificial Intelligence at California Baptist University

California Baptist University (CBU) is committed to the responsible, ethical, and secure use of artificial intelligence (AI) to support teaching, learning, research, and institutional operations while maintaining human accountability, academic integrity, and alignment with the university’s mission.

Institutional Guidance

AI tools may enhance productivity and insight, but they must be used in ways that align with university policy, data‑classification requirements, and institutional values. Enterprise‑approved platforms provide safeguards that consumer tools do not.

Key Principles for AI Use at CBU

  • Use only ITS‑approved enterprise AI tools for university‑related work
  • Protect student, employee, and institutional data
  • Maintain human oversight, judgment, and accountability
  • Align AI use with academic integrity and the CBU mission

University‑Approved AI Tools

California Baptist University maintains a defined list of artificial intelligence tools that have been reviewed and approved by Information Technology Services (ITS). Approval reflects enterprise security, privacy, and contractual safeguards.

Executive Guidance

Approval does not imply unrestricted use. Users remain fully accountable for decisions, outputs, and outcomes supported by AI.

Responsible Use of Artificial Intelligence Policy

This policy establishes expectations for the ethical, secure, and responsible use of artificial intelligence for university‑related activities.

  • Use only ITS‑approved AI tools, including Microsoft Copilot (Enterprise / Edu)
  • Safeguard confidential, regulated, and sensitive data
  • Maintain meaningful human oversight of AI‑assisted outputs
  • Comply with academic integrity, legal, and regulatory requirements
