Request AI Tool Review (ITS)

California Baptist University requires that artificial intelligence (AI) tools used for university-related work be reviewed and approved by Information Technology Services (ITS) prior to adoption or use.

This review process helps ensure AI tools align with institutional security standards, data-protection requirements, ethical expectations, and regulatory obligations.

When a Review Is Required

  • The AI tool is not listed as university-approved
  • The proposed use involves internal, sensitive, or regulated data
  • The tool impacts students, employees, or institutional decisions
  • A pilot, evaluation, or departmental rollout is being considered

What the Review Considers

  • Data protection, privacy, and security controls
  • Legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements
  • Ethical considerations and institutional risk
  • Appropriateness of the proposed use case

How to Initiate a Review

To begin an AI tool review, contact Information Technology Services with a brief description of:

  • The AI tool or platform being considered
  • The intended use case
  • The type of data involved
  • The expected users or scope of use

ITS will provide guidance on next steps, including any required documentation, risk assessment, or staged approval process.
