Contact Information Technology Services

Information Technology Services (ITS) supports California Baptist University faculty, staff, and students in the secure and responsible use of technology, including artificial intelligence.

ITS serves as the primary point of contact for questions related to AI tool approval, data protection, security standards, and technical guidance for university‑related AI use.

Contact ITS For Assistance With:

  • Questions about university‑approved AI tools
  • Requests to review new AI tools or pilot projects
  • Guidance on data classification and appropriate AI use
  • Technical access issues related to AI platforms
  • Coordination with other governance or compliance offices

How to Reach ITS

Faculty, staff, and students should use established CBU IT support channels to contact Information Technology Services, such as the ITS website or service request system.

When submitting AI‑related questions or requests, providing a clear description of the intended use and data involved will help ITS route the request appropriately.
